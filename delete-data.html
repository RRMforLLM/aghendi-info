Data Deletion Policy for Aghendi

This document outlines the process for requesting the deletion of your personal data from Aghendi, even if you don't have an active account or want to delete specific data while maintaining your account.

How to Request Data Deletion

Aghendi respects your privacy rights and provides several options to request deletion of your personal data:

Option 1: Data Deletion from within the App

  1. Open the Aghendi application
  2. Navigate to Settings (gear icon in the top right) of your profile
  3. Select "Privacy & Data"
  4. Tap on "Manage My Data"
  5. Choose "Delete Specific Data" or "Delete All Data"
  6. Follow the prompts to specify what data you want deleted
  7. Confirm your choice

Option 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app or prefer to make your request via email:

  1. Send an email to aghendi.mail@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request"
  2. Include the email address associated with your data
  3. Specify what data you want deleted (e.g., "all data," "photos only," "usage history")
  4. We may ask for additional information to verify your identity
  5. Once verified, we will process your deletion request within 7 business days

Important: Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you choose to delete all your data while keeping your account, you will need to re-upload or re-create any content you wish to continue using.

Types of Data That Can Be Deleted

Personal Information

You can request deletion of various types of personal information:

  • Profile information (name, email, profile picture)
  • Contact information
  • Device information
  • Location data

User-Generated Content

You can request deletion of content you've created or uploaded:

  • Photos and images stored on our servers
  • Comments and interactions
  • Custom settings and preferences

Activity and Usage Data

You can request deletion of data related to your activity:

  • Usage history and logs
  • Search history
  • Behavioral data and preferences

Data Deletion Process

Timeline for Deletion

After your data deletion request is received and verified:

  • We will begin processing your request within 2 business days
  • Most data will be deleted from active systems within 7 business days
  • Data in backup systems will be deleted within 30 days
  • You will receive confirmation when the deletion is complete

Data That May Be Retained

Even after a deletion request, certain information may be retained for the following purposes:

  • Aggregated Analytics: Non-personally identifiable, anonymized data used for app analytics and improvements may be retained indefinitely.
  • Legal Compliance: Information necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements may be retained for up to 2 years.
  • Transaction Records: If you've made purchases, we may retain transaction records as required by tax and accounting laws.

For Non-Account Users

If you don't have an Aghendi account but believe we may have collected your data (for example, through cookies or other tracking technologies):

  1. Email aghendi.mail@gmail.com with the subject "Non-Account Data Deletion"
  2. Provide information about when and how your data might have been collected
  3. Include any identifiers that might help us locate your data (device type, approximate dates, etc.)

Data Portability

Before deleting your data, you may request a copy of your personal information in a commonly used, machine-readable format. To request a data export:

  1. Follow the same process as the data deletion request
  2. Specify that you want to export your data before deletion
  3. Allow up to 14 days for us to prepare your data export

Contact Us

If you have any questions about data deletion or need assistance, you can contact us:

  • By email: aghendi.mail@gmail.com
