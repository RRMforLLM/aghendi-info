About Aghendi

Aghendi is a versatile application designed to help users organize, manage, and enhance their digital experience. Our platform offers intuitive features while maintaining strong privacy protections for all our users.

Getting Started

To get started with Aghendi, download our application from your preferred app store and create an account. You can sign up using your email or through Google or Instagram authentication.

Note: Aghendi is committed to protecting your privacy and providing transparent information about how we handle your data. Please review our policies below.

Our Policies

We believe in transparency and providing clear information about how we handle your data. Please take a moment to review our policies:

  • Privacy Policy - Details about how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

  • Data Deletion Policy - Information about how to request deletion of specific data while maintaining your account.

  • Account Deletion Policy - Instructions for completely removing your account and associated data from our systems.

Features

Aghendi offers a range of features designed to enhance your experience:

  • Easy photo management and organization
  • Seamless integration with other platforms
  • User-friendly interface
  • Privacy-focused design
  • Customizable settings

Account Management

Managing your Aghendi account is simple. Within the application, navigate to the Settings section to:

  • Update your profile information
  • Adjust privacy settings
  • Manage your data
  • Review connected third-party services
