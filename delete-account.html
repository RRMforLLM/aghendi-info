Account Deletion Policy for Aghendi

This document outlines the process for requesting account deletion from Aghendi, the types of data that will be deleted or retained, and information about any additional retention periods.

How to Request Account Deletion

Aghendi values your privacy and provides a straightforward process to delete your account and associated personal data:

Option 1: Delete Account from within the App

  1. Open the Aghendi application
  2. Navigate to Settings (gear icon in the top right) of your profile
  3. Scroll down and, at the bottom of the page, tap on "Delete Account"
  4. Read the information about account deletion
  5. Confirm your choice

Option 2: Email Request

If you cannot access your account or prefer to make your request via email:

  1. Send an email to aghendi.mail@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"
  2. Include the email address associated with your Aghendi account
  3. We may ask for additional information to verify your identity
  4. Once verified, we will process your deletion request within 7 business days

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your personal data associated with the account will be deleted as described below.

Data Deletion Information

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon approval of your account deletion request, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • Your account profile information (name, email, profile picture)
  • Photos and images uploaded to our servers
  • Usage history and activity logs
  • Device information previously collected
  • Any content you've created within the app
  • Contact information

Data That May Be Retained

Certain information may be retained even after account deletion for the following purposes:

  • Aggregated Analytics: Non-personally identifiable, anonymized data used for app analytics and improvements may be retained indefinitely.
  • Legal Compliance: Information necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements may be retained for up to 2 years from the date of account deletion.
  • Backup Data: Your information may remain in our backup systems for up to 30 days after deletion before being permanently removed.

Retention Periods

After you request account deletion:

  • Most of your personal data will be deleted within 7 business days
  • Backup data containing your information will be deleted within 30 days
  • Data retained for legal purposes will be deleted after 2 years

Additional Information

Third-Party Data

If you created your account using Google or Instagram, we will stop accessing and using data from these services. However, you may need to review your connection settings in those services to remove any remaining permissions.

Impact on Active Subscriptions

If you have an active subscription, deleting your account will not automatically cancel your subscription. Please cancel any active subscriptions before deleting your account by visiting the Subscriptions section in your app store account settings.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about account deletion or need assistance, you can contact us:

  • By email: aghendi.mail@gmail.com
